Primo

  • Primo is the KTH Library search tool where you search for publications that the library has on its shelves or that are offered in electronic form.

  • To refine your search to printed material available in the library, please use the "Printed materials" filter, which you find just above the search results.

  • Please note that from this public computer you can only search, but you cannot access online material.

  • The complete version of this search tool is available through the library website.

  • Please note that the browser will reset after 3 minutes of inactivity.
